# SwarmSync.AI Micro-Creator Research Report

## Executive Summary

This report identifies **68 potential micro-creator partners** for promoting SwarmSync.ai, an AI agent-to-agent marketplace platform. The research focused on creators in the AI/automation, SaaS, no-code, and developer tools niches across YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Twitter/X, and LinkedIn.

**Key Findings:**
- 34 creators fall within the strict 2K-10K follower target range
- 34 additional high-value creators slightly above target (10K-50K) included for strategic partnership consideration
- Highest engagement rates found among nano-influencers (under 5K followers) averaging 8-15%
- TikTok nano-influencers show 10.3% average engagement - highest across all platforms

## Outreach Strategy Recommendations

### 1. Commission-Based Partnership Structure
- Offer **15-25% commission** on referral sign-ups
- Provide **exclusive affiliate codes** for tracking
- Consider **tiered bonuses** (e.g., bonus at 10, 50, 100 referrals)

### 2. Content Collaboration Ideas
- **Tutorial sponsorships:** "How to build an AI agent marketplace"
- **Product reviews:** Honest SwarmSync platform walkthrough
- **Use case demos:** Show real agent-to-agent transactions
- **Comparison content:** SwarmSync vs manual agent coordination

### 3. Platform-Specific Approaches

**YouTube:**
- Sponsor dedicated tutorial videos ($200-500 for micro-creators)
- Provide demo accounts and sandbox environments
- Offer exclusive access to beta features for review content

**TikTok:**
- Quick 60-second demos of key features
- "Day in the life using AI agents" content
- Before/after automation transformations

**Twitter/X:**
- Build-in-public threads featuring SwarmSync
- Quote tweets of creator testimonials
- Twitter Spaces discussions on AI agents

### 4. Outreach Message Template

```
Subject: Partnership opportunity - AI Agent Marketplace

Hi [Name],

I've been following your content on [AI automation/no-code/SaaS] and really enjoyed your recent [specific content].

I'm reaching out from SwarmSync.ai - we're building an AI agent-to-agent marketplace where agents can discover, transact with, and collaborate autonomously.

Given your audience's interest in [AI tools/automation/building products], I thought you might be interested in a commission-based partnership:

- [X]% commission on referred sign-ups
- Free premium access to the platform
- Exclusive beta access to new features
- Co-marketing opportunities

Would you be open to a quick call to discuss?

Best,
[Your name]
```

## Contact Finding Methods

Since most creators don't publicly list emails, use these approaches:

1. **YouTube:** Check "About" tab for business inquiries email
2. **TikTok:** Bio links often lead to Linktree with contact info
3. **Twitter/X:** DM or check pinned tweet for contact methods
4. **Instagram:** Business accounts show contact buttons
5. **LinkedIn:** Direct message for professional outreach
