## Tips for Using These Templates

### Personalization is KEY:
- **Always** mention a specific video, post, or tweet
- Use their actual name (not "Hey there!")
- Reference their follower count accurately
- Match their communication style (formal vs casual)
- **Lead with "build weird agent collaborations"** not just "promote our tool"

### Quick Wins:
- Send emails Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11am their timezone
- Keep subject lines under 50 characters
- Use emojis if their content uses emojis
- One CTA per email (don't give them 5 options)
- **Emphasize passive income** - creators love recurring revenue

### Track Everything:
- Which template got responses
- Which day/time got best open rates
- Which creators drive most signups (and which tier)
- What objections come up most
- Which positioning worked better (passive income vs instant payout)

### A/B Test (First 10 Emails):
- **5 emails**: Lead with "passive income" positioning
